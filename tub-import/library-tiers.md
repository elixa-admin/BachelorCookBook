# TUB — Tiered Library Roster (DRAFT v1, 2026-06-28)

96 dishes across 5 tiers. SA naming throughout. Supersedes flat `shortlist-100.md`.
**Tier = prominence/adventure axis; orthogonal to occasion (solo/date/host) and category.**

## Tier philosophy
- **Signature 12** — "The ones that define you." Brand anchors. Master these and you eat
  extraordinarily. Highest repeatability + broad technique coverage (sear, braise, roast,
  emulsion, curry, bake, griddle). Date-led.
- **Premium 24** — "Cook to impress." Dinner-party / close-the-evening grade. Elevated
  technique, cost or plating payoff. The hosting tier.
- **Global Favorites 24** — "The world's most loved." Universal, recognisable, consensus
  crowd-pleasers. Guests say "oh, I love this."
- **Exotic 24** — "Show your range." Adventurous / regional / unfamiliar. The gap-fill tier
  (Korean, Vietnamese, Greek, African-beyond-SA, Moroccan, Peruvian, Indonesian).
- **Others 12** — "The supporting cast." Desserts, breads, brunch, sides, techniques that
  complete a meal or an evening.

Occasion target across all 96: ~50% solo / ~35% date / ~15% host.
