## Coverage check

- **Techniques:** sear/baste, steam, skin-crisp, bake/dessert, roast, emulsion, temper-egg, risotto/mount, smash/fry, curry/layer, braise, simmer-eggs.
- **Reach:** French, Italian, Nordic/modern, American, Thai, Mexican, North African.
- **Gear:** all bachelor-achievable (one pan / pot / oven); no specialist equipment required.
