# Molten Chocolate Fondant

> Make dessert once and you'll make it forever. These can be filled and chilled hours
> ahead, then baked to order while you clear the plates — twelve minutes from fridge to a
> dark, runny centre that makes the whole meal feel planned.

## Ingredients

- 100g dark chocolate, 70%
- 100g butter
- 2 whole eggs + 2 yolks
- 50g caster sugar
- 30g plain flour
- Cocoa, to dust
- Pinch of salt

*Doubles and quadruples cleanly for a crowd.*

## Steps

1. Butter two ramekins well and dust with cocoa.
2. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
3. Whisk the eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale.
4. Fold the chocolate into the eggs, then fold in the flour and salt.
5. Divide between the ramekins. Bake at 200°C for 10–11 minutes — set at the edges,
   still wobbling in the centre.
6. Turn out immediately. To prep ahead, chill after step 5 and add 1–2 minutes to the
   bake.

## The move

Underbake on purpose. Edges set, centre molten — if you're unsure, ten minutes, not twelve.

## To drink

An espresso, or a nip of bourbon beside it. Non-alc: good coffee, no sugar.
