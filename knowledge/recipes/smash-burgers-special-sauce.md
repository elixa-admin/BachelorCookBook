# Smash Burgers with Special Sauce

> The smash burger is the most reliably excellent thing you can make for a crowd — a
> thin, crispy-edged patty, melted cheese, and a sauce you'll end up putting on
> everything. Double the buns; people always want another.

## Ingredients

- 500g ground beef, ~20% fat, kept loose
- 4 brioche buns
- 4 slices of cheese (American or cheddar)
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- Pickles, to serve

**Special sauce:** 4 tbsp mayo, 1 tbsp ketchup, 1 tsp mustard, a splash of pickle juice,
a pinch of paprika.

*Per serving: ~125g beef.*

## Steps

1. Mix the sauce and set aside.
2. Form the beef into 4 loose balls — don't pack them.
3. Get the pan smoking. Place a ball, smash it flat and thin with a spatula within ten
   seconds, salt it, and cook 2 minutes until the edges crisp.
4. Flip, top with cheese, and cook 1 minute more.
5. Toast the buns. Build: sauce, patty, onion, pickles.

## The move

Smash thin, once, within the first ten seconds — you're making a crispy edge, not a puck.

## To drink

A cold lager. Non-alc: a vanilla shake.
