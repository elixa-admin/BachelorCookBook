# Spaghetti Carbonara

> Real carbonara has no cream — just eggs, cured pork, cheese, and pepper, turned to silk
> with the pasta's own heat. Twenty minutes, and it's the dish that proves you actually
> know how to cook.

## Ingredients

- 200g spaghetti
- 100g guanciale or pancetta, diced
- 2 whole eggs + 1 yolk
- 50g pecorino romano, grated
- Lots of black pepper

*Per serving: 100g pasta, 50g guanciale, 1 egg.*

## Steps

1. Crisp the guanciale in a dry pan; leave every drop of fat.
2. Whisk the eggs, yolk, pecorino, and a generous grind of pepper into a paste.
3. Cook the pasta al dente; reserve a mug of water before draining.
4. Off the heat, add the hot pasta to the guanciale, then the egg mixture and a splash of
   water. Toss fast and continuously — the residual heat sets it to a creamy sauce.
5. Finish with more pepper and more cheese.

## The move

Off the heat before the egg goes in, or you get scrambled. Residual heat is all you need.

## To drink

A white or a beer. Non-alc: sparkling water.
