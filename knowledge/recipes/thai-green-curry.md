# Thai Green Curry

> A good curry is all about layering — aromatics first, then richness, then balance at
> the end. Once you can taste sweet, salty, sour, and heat in a single spoonful, you can
> cook half of Asia.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp green curry paste
- 1 × 400ml tin coconut milk
- 2 chicken thighs, sliced
- 1 aubergine or 2 peppers, chunked
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- Juice of 1 lime
- Kaffir lime leaves or Thai basil
- Jasmine rice, to serve

*Per serving: 1 thigh, 200ml coconut milk.*

## Steps

1. Spoon the thick cream off the top of the coconut milk and simmer it in a hot pot for
   2 minutes until it splits.
2. Fry the curry paste in that cream for 2 minutes, until deeply fragrant.
3. Add the chicken and toss to coat.
4. Pour in the remaining coconut milk and the veg; simmer 10 minutes until thickened.
5. Season with fish sauce, sugar, and lime. Taste and adjust until it's balanced — sweet,
   salty, sour, heat all present.
6. Serve over rice, scattered with basil or torn lime leaves.

## The move

Split the coconut — fry the paste in the thick cream first. It deepens the flavour before
the milk ever goes in.

## To drink

A lager, or jasmine tea. Non-alc: jasmine tea.
