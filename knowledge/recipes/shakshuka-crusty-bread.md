# Shakshuka with Crusty Bread

> Shakshuka is the answer to almost any meal — eggs cradled in a spiced tomato sauce,
> scooped up with bread, cheap and beautiful in equal measure. Breakfast, midnight, or a
> quiet night in; it always feels like more effort than it is.

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 red pepper, sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp paprika, a pinch of chilli
- 1 × 400g tin tomatoes
- 4 eggs
- Feta, optional
- Parsley or coriander, to finish
- Crusty bread, to serve

*Per serving: 2 eggs.*

## Steps

1. Soften the onion and pepper in the oil, about 6 minutes.
2. Add the garlic and spices; cook 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add the tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes until thick. Season well.
4. Make four wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each.
5. Cover and cook gently 5 minutes, until the whites are set and the yolks still soft.
6. Scatter with feta and herbs. Serve straight from the pan with bread.

## The move

Reduce the sauce until it's thick before the eggs go in — otherwise they poach into soup.

## To drink

A Bloody Mary at brunch, or mint tea at night. Non-alc: mint tea.
